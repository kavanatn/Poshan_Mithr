What is Poshan Mithr?

A comprehensive platform that bridges schools, anganwadis, parents, and district officials with real-time nutrition analytics, BMI tracking, and personalized dietary recommendations

Poshan Mithr transforms nutrition management across schools and anganwadis by providing intelligent insights into child health and meal quality. Our platform empowers stakeholders with data-driven decisions, ensuring every child receives optimal nutrition for healthy growth and development. Built on modern technology and grounded in nutritional science, we're reshaping how educational institutions and early childhood centers approach student wellness.
